## Controller
*Warning: The buttons respond for however long the button is held*
### Movements:
- Up (17)
- Down (22)
- Right (12)
- Left (4)
### Actions:
- A (21)
- B (20)
- Esc (5)
- Use (6)

## Alt Controller
*Used for Etch-a-Sketch and Whack a Mole*
- 4x2 lego brick style
- Esc/Use column

## Keyboard *for testing*
- WASD (movements)
- Space (use)
- Q (A1)
- E (A2)
- R (esc)
